Inserting a Variable

 

  1. Open your topic or other content file in the XML Editor.

 

  1. Place your cursor in the location where you would like to insert the variable.

 

  1. Open the Project Organizer window.

 

  1. Double click on the Variables Folder to expand.

 

  1. Expand the appropriate variable set.

 

  1. Locate the desired variable. Click and drag the variable to the location in the content where you left the cursor.

 

  1. Click File/Save.
