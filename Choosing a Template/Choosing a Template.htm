Choosing a Template

 

In the Start New Project Wizard, there is a list of templates. They are divided into Online, Online & Print, and Print. Online & Print is the most commonly used option, but you can use whichever best fits your needs. For the sake of this tutorial, we will be using Online & Print. This means that you will be producing two outputs, a PDF and a website. We’ll get into outputs later; for now, let’s focus on picking a template.

 

  1. Open the Online & Print folder if it is not already open. There are four options listed. Two of them are advanced and two are basic. For now we will be using basic templates. In this tutorial, we will be using Top Navigation and PDF Basic, but the choice is ultimately yours.

 

  1. Click on Top Navigation and PDF Basic.

 

  1. Click Next. A dialogue box appears, which lists information about HTML5.  If you scroll down, it will give you information about Adobe PDF. You do not need to worry about this right now. Make sure that Available Targets at the top of the screen is set to HTML5.

 

  1. Click Finish. You now have a template where you can begin to add content.
