Beginning a Project

 

  1. Click the language that you prefer in the Select UI Language dialogue box.

 

  1. Click on New Project in the Getting Started section. The Start New Project Wizard appears.

 

  1. Under Project Name, choose a title for your project.

 

  1. Under Project Folder, choose the folder on your computer where you would like your project to be saved.

 

  1. Click Next. You now need to choose a template. This may seem intimidating, since there are quite a few to choose from, but we will help keep you on track.

 

Fill up:

Take a look at the interface. MadCap Flare is great for beginners, since it gives you many different options for help. At the top of the main interface, there is a tab called Tutorials, and another called Help Resources. The Tutorials tab provides video tutorials to help you learn Flare as you go. The Help Resources tab gives you access to guides and other web resources. On the right side of the screen, there is a panel called Dynamic Help, which is a guide that helps you as you explore Flare. When you click on something, the guide will change to explain what you clicked on.
